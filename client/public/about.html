About Peskaya

Welcome to Peskaya! Our platform is dedicated to helping you find your perfect home with ease. Users can explore houses listed on our platform for free, complete with detailed information and location insights. For more personalized searches — if a home you want isn't listed — our team offers a paid custom search service where we go out and find it for you.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make house hunting easier, faster, and more accessible. Whether you're browsing available listings or requesting a custom search, we are committed to providing a simple and reliable experience, helping you find a home that truly fits your needs.

How It Works

Browse houses freely through our platform, view location details, and explore options. If you can’t find what you're looking for, you can request a custom search — our team will personally scout for properties based on your specific requirements. We also offer a marketplace where you can shop for house-related items like decor, furniture, and more, with secure payments handled directly through our platform.

Why Choose Us?

  • Free access to listed houses with location information
  • Custom house searches tailored to your specific needs
  • Convenient marketplace for home decor and essentials
  • Secure and reliable payment system
  • Dedicated team focused on delivering results
  • Simple, user-friendly platform for all your home needs

Contact Us

If you have any questions or need assistance, feel free to reach out to us at support@peskaya.com. We are here to make your house hunting and home shopping experience as smooth as possible.
